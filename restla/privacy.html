Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026 · Last updated: July 3, 2026

Restla ("we", "our", "us") is a 20-20-20 eye break timer app developed by Ümit Can Erim. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Data stored locally on your device (never sent to our servers):

  • Timer settings (work interval, break duration)
  • Usage statistics (number of breaks taken, streaks)
  • App preferences and Pro purchase status

Restla does not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information.

2. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob (free users only): We display ads via Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Apple / Google Play: In-app purchases are processed by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android). We do not receive or store your payment information.

3. Notifications

Restla uses local notifications to remind you to take eye breaks. These notifications are scheduled on your device and do not transmit any data externally. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

4. Analytics

We do not use any analytics SDKs. We do not track how you use the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Restla is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data by uninstalling Restla.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete remotely. All your data lives on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
