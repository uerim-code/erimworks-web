How do lives work?

Each level attempt costs 1 life. Lives regenerate automatically — one life every 30 minutes. You can also purchase extra lives or watch an ad to earn a free life.

I made a purchase but didn't receive my items

Please wait a few seconds and restart the app. If the issue persists, use the "Restore Purchases" option in the Shop. If you still have trouble, contact us with your order ID.

How do I restore my progress?

Your progress is saved automatically on your device. If you reinstall the app or switch devices, your progress may not transfer at this time. We're working on cloud save support.

How do boosters work?

Boosters are powerful tools you can use during a level. Hammer removes a single tile, Rocket clears a row or column, Bomb clears an area, and Rainbow removes all tiles of one color.

How do I cancel my VIP subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your App Store or Google Play account settings. Go to Subscriptions and cancel Match Manor VIP. You'll keep access until the end of the billing period.
