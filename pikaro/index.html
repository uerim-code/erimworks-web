How do I remove ads?

Open the Profile tab and tap "Upgrade to Pikaro Premium" or "Pikaro Ad-Free". Both are auto-renewable subscriptions managed through your Apple ID or Google account under Settings → Subscriptions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.
Android: Google Play Store → Subscriptions.

Does "Create from photo" upload my photos anywhere?

No. Photos are processed entirely on your device and never leave it.
